Exclusions & Void Conditions

What’s Not Covered

This warranty does not cover issues outside ProCoat’s control, including:

  • Concrete movement, cracking, or settling
  • Structural shifts or drainage issues
  • Moisture, hydrostatic pressure, or water intrusion
  • Damage from chemicals, heavy objects, or misuse
  • Acts of nature (storms, floods, etc.)
  • Normal wear, fading, or cosmetic changes
  • Damage from other contractors or external factors

Cracks in existing concrete may reappear over time.

Warranty Void Conditions

Coverage may be void if:

  • Unauthorized repairs or alterations are made
  • Damage results from misuse or neglect
  • Issues are not reported promptly
  • Inspection or repair access is denied
  • Project is unpaid or incomplete

Non-covered issues may still be repairable at your expense.
