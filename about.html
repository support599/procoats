About Pro Coat Concrete Coatings

We’re Not Just Resurfacing Concrete.We’re Raising the Standard.

Pro Coat was built around a simple idea: homeowners deserve better options for their outdoor spaces. Too many pool decks, patios, and concrete surfaces are hot, cracked, slippery, outdated, or covered with coatings that peel and fail after only a few years. We knew there had to be a better way — so we built a company around finding it.

Where We’re From

Built in East Texas. Growing Across the Region.

Pro Coat is headquartered in Tyler, Texas, and proudly serves homeowners throughout Texas and Louisiana.

What started as a local concrete coatings company has grown into a specialized team completing projects across East Texas, Dallas–Fort Worth, College Station, Waco, Texarkana, Shreveport, and the surrounding areas.

Our growth has come from one thing above all else: homeowners telling other homeowners about their experience with Pro Coat.

Our Focus

We Specialize in Something Different

We don’t try to be everything to everyone. Our specialty is transforming worn-out pool decks, patios, and concrete surfaces with premium surfacing systems designed to look better, feel better, and last longer.

  • Our signature Pro-Flex™ rubber surfacing system is especially popular around pools because it creates a softer, more comfortable surface underfoot while completely transforming the look of the space.
  • For garages, shops, and other concrete areas, we also install professional-grade flake floor coating systems built for durability and everyday use.

Every project starts with the same goal: make the space look dramatically better — and install it the right way.
