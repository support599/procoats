Why Homeowners Choose Pro-Flex

Cooler Under the Sun

Concrete can become unbearably hot during the summer. Pro-Flex stays noticeably cooler, making it far more comfortable for bare feet — even on the hottest Texas days.

Softer & More Comfortable

The resilient rubber surface provides a cushioned feel that reduces fatigue and creates a more enjoyable space for kids, pets, and adults alike.

Slip-Resistant

The textured finish helps provide excellent traction around wet pool areas, giving your family greater confidence with every step.

Built to Last

Pro-Flex is engineered to resist cracking, peeling, and fading while standing up to harsh weather, UV exposure, and heavy foot traffic.

Beautiful Custom Designs

Choose from a wide selection of colors and blends to create a custom look that complements your home, pool, and outdoor living space.

Low Maintenance

No constant repainting or frequent repairs. Pro-Flex is easy to clean and designed to keep your pool deck looking great for years.
