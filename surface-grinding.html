Proper Prep, Every Time

Why Concrete Grinding Matters

Grinding removes old materials and opens the surface so new coatings bond properly.

Skipping this step can cause peeling and failure.

Our Equipment

Commercial-Grade Grinding Equipment

We use commercial-grade grinding equipment to:

  • Remove paint and coatings
  • Strip epoxy
  • Remove glue and residue
  • Smooth concrete
  • Prep surfaces for new coatings

When Is Grinding Needed?

Grinding is recommended when:

Old Coatings Need Removal

Surface Has Sealers or Residue

Better Adhesion Is Needed
