Why Texarkana Homeowners Choose Pro-Flex™

Texas summers are hard on traditional concrete — cracking, fading, and getting dangerously hot underfoot. Pro-Flex™ rubber surfacing stays noticeably cooler, resists cracking, and keeps its color for years, even in Texarkana’s toughest weather.
